How to Protect Yourself
📱

Social Media Scams

Fake Instagram vendors, Twitter giveaway scams, Facebook marketplace fraud. Always check a seller's handle before sending money.

💰

Investment Scams

Ponzi schemes, fake crypto platforms, "double your money" offers. If returns sound too good to be true — they are.

🏠

Rental Scams

Fake landlords collecting rent for property they don't own. Always verify ownership at the land registry before payment.

💼

Job Scams

Fake job offers requiring upfront payment for training or processing fees. Legitimate employers do not charge to hire you.

🛡️ About TrustCheck Nigeria

TrustCheck Nigeria is a community-powered scam reporting and business verification platform built to protect Nigerians from online fraud.

Nigeria loses billions of naira every year to online scams. There was no single searchable place where Nigerians could instantly check if a phone number, business, or account had been reported. TrustCheck fills that gap.

What makes us different

Unlike government portals that only tell you if a business is registered, TrustCheck combines CAC government verification with community social proof.

How the Trust Score works: Our score (0–100) uses report count, unique reporters, star ratings, and vote credibility. A business with 50 reports from 50 people scores very differently from one with 50 reports from the same account.
